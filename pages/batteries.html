SIGNS YOU MAY NEED A NEW BATTERY:

  • Slow engine start or multiple attempts to start the vehicle.
  • Dim headlights, even when the engine is running.

  • Swollen or bloated battery case.
  • Sulfuric or rotten egg odor coming from the battery.
