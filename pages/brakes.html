Ensure Your Vehicle Stops Safely!

Your safety and that of your passengers are of utmost importance, and functioning brakes play a critical role. At TireTrack, we prioritize getting your brake repairs done right the first time. Delaying brake pad or rotor replacement can cause extensive damage to your braking system, resulting in higher repair costs down the line. To save money, protect your vehicle, and prevent long-term damage, it's essential to address brake issues promptly. Trust us for professional brake repairs that ensure optimal performance and the safety of you and your loved ones.

SIGNS YOU NEED TO HAVE YOUR BRAKES INSPECTED:

  • Squeaking or squealing noises: If you hear high-pitched noises when applying the brakes, it could be an indication of worn brake pads.
  • Vibrations or pulsations: If you feel vibrations or pulsations in the brake pedal when you apply pressure, it could suggest issues with the brake rotors.
  • Longer braking distance: If you notice that it takes longer to stop your vehicle than usual, it could be a sign of worn brake pads or a brake system problem.
  • Warning light: If the brake warning light on your dashboard is illuminated, it's essential to have your brakes inspected as soon as possible.
